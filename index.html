FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Some of your questions

1. Do I need to sync my mobile every day with SleepDoc+?

No, SleepDoc+ and mobile works independently. It stores data on the internal memory and syncs with mobile phone periodically. However, if the phone is not available, it will keep the data until it synced with the mobile phone.

2. Does it record room conversation too?

No way, it only records the level of sound, we understand your privacy.

3. How will I get services if there is something wrong with my device?

We have made SleepDoc+ in such a way you do not need to engage with your device. However, nothing is 100% perfect, so that if you get some problem with your device you can contact us at support@mbreath.com or you can create an online support ticket at http://www.mbreath.com/help-and-support.

4. Will it be available on e-commerce sites like Flipkart, Amazon, Snapdeal etc.?

Yes, you can order SleepDoc+ from e-commerce websites as well as from mBreath’s site.
