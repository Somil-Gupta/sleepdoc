Ideally how many hours should a man sleep, so that he is healthy?

Sleep needs vary across ages and are especially impacted by lifestyle and health. To determine how much sleep you need, it's important to assess not only where you fall on the "sleep needs spectrum," but also to examine what lifestyle factors are affecting the quality and quantity of your sleep such as work schedules and stress.

Generally we need 8 hours of sleep for adults but different age group need different amount of sleep.

The detailed schedule of sleep time for different age groups are as follows:-

  • Newborns (0-3 months): 14-17 hours each day
  • Infants (4-11 months): 12-15 hours each day
  • Toddlers (1-2 years): 11-14 hours each day
  • Preschoolers (3-5): 10-13 hours each day
  • School age children (6-13): 9-11 hours each day
  • Teenagers (14-17): 8-10 hours each day
  • Younger adults (18-25): 7-9 hours each day
  • Adults (26-64): 7-9 hours each day
  • Older adults (65+): 7-8 hours each day

This picture show the average sleep time of individual of different age group by National sleep foundation

Some of the problems that might happen due to sleep deprivation are:

  • 1. It may also hamper your reproductive health and decrease your immune system
  • 2. It also impacts your heart muscles and hence can be the cause of cardiovascular diseases
  • 3. It causes obesity
  • 4. Affects your memory and reduce your thinking abilities
  • 5. Causes lack of alertness and increase irritability

How to fight this:-

  • 1. stick to a sleep schedule
  • 2. Exercise daily
  • 3. Evaluate your bedroom to ensure ideal temperature ,sound and light
  • 4. Sleep on a comfortable mattress and pillows
  • 5. Reduce caffeine and alcohol consumption
  • 6. Turn off electronics at least 1 hour before bed

    Is sleep a state of complete unconsciousness, or is it merely subconsciousness?

    Our mind is dived into 3 parts namely conscious (10%), subconscious (50–60%) and unconscious (30–40%)
    The conscious mind communicates to the outside world and the inner self through speech, writing , physical movements and thought.
    Subconscious mind is in charge of recent memories and is in continuous contact with the resources of the unconscious mind.
    Unconscious mind is the storehouse of all the memories and past experiences.

    So consciousness means to be aware and respond to outside stimulus and unconscious means to be unaware and unresponsive.
    Normal sleep is an altered state of consciousness. You are aware and responding to stimuli , such as rolling over when uncomfortable.
